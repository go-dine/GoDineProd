# GoDine — Complete Guide for Restaurant Owners

Welcome to **GoDine**! This simple guide will walk you through setting up your digital menu, generating QR codes for your tables, and managing live customer orders.

---

## 1. Accessing Your Dashboard

The **Dashboard** is your control center. You do not need to install any app; you can access it directly from any web browser on a laptop, tablet, or phone.
- Simply ask your system setup person for the **Dashboard Link** (e.g., `www.yourwebsite.com/dashboard.html`).

## 2. Registering Your Restaurant

If this is your first time using GoDine:
1. Open the Dashboard link.
2. Click on the **"Register here"** button.
3. Fill in your details:
   - **Restaurant Name:** (e.g., *The Cosmic Cafe*)
   - **Unique Link (Slug):** A short, simple word for your menu link (e.g., *cosmic*). No spaces allowed!
   - **Password:** Keep this safe! You will need it to log in again.
   - **Number of Tables:** How many tables you have in your dining area.
4. Click **Register**. You will automatically be logged in!

*Next time you visit, simply enter your "Unique Link" and "Password" on the main page to log back in.*

---

## 3. Creating Your Digital Menu

Once logged in, you will be taken to the **Menu** tab.
Here is where you add all the delicious items you serve:
1. On the left side under "Add New Dish", enter the details:
   - **Name:** The name of the dish.
   - **Description:** A tasty description of the ingredients.
   - **Price:** Just the number (e.g., `250`).
   - **Category:** Choose whether it's a Starter, Main Course, Beverage, or Dessert.
   - **Emoji:** Pick a fun emoji to make your menu pop! 🍔 ☕ 🥗
2. Click **Add Dish**.
3. It will immediately appear in your menu list! You can easily **Edit** or **Delete** dishes later using the buttons next to each item on the right.

---

## 4. Getting Your QR Codes

Customers will scan a QR code on their table to see your menu and order without waiting for a waiter. Here is how you get them:
1. Click on the **QR Codes** tab at the top of your Dashboard.
2. You will see a unique QR code generated for every single table in your restaurant.
3. **Print this page!** You can use your browser’s print function (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P on your keyboard) to print the codes on standard paper or sticker sheets.
4. Cut them out and stick each QR code onto its matching physical table in your restaurant.

---

## 5. Taking Live Orders (The Kitchen View)

Now for the best part! Keep your tablet or computer open to the **Orders** tab during business hours.

When a customer scans the QR code on Table 5 and places a food order, it will pop up on your screen instantly. Here is how to manage them:
1. When a new order arrives, it shows up as **🟡 Pending**. You will see the table number and the exact items they ordered.
2. Update the order status as your kitchen works on it:
   - Click **Prepare** when the chefs start cooking (it turns **🟠 Preparing**).
   - Click **Ready** when the food is ready to be picked up by the waiter (it turns **🟢 Ready**).
   - Click **Complete** when the food is delivered to the table (it turns **🔵 Completed** and moves out of the way).

That's it! By keeping the Orders tab updated, you ensure the kitchen stays organized and your customers get their food blazing fast. Enjoy running your digital restaurant!
